Welcome to Our Assassin Game

Assassin is a live-actions game. Each Player must eliminate each other from the game using specific weapons. The last Player standing will be crowned the winner!!!

Setting Up:

Step 1A: Please Register Your Email and Password. If you already have an account, please just login.

Step 1B: You will be directed to a new page containing your login information. Head back to the homepage and login.

Step 2: If all goes well, you will be redirected to your profile page. Your profile page contains statistic of your kills along with the object to make a new game or continue a current game
